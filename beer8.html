river city

raspberry ale

Our best-selling beer, River City Raspberry, is a fruit beer that builds on our already delicious blonde ale by adding a locally blended 100% natural raspberry purée that lends a subtle sophisticated tang. Incredibly easy-drinking, it’s served in our restaurants with a spoonful of raspberries in every pint.

  • Colour :
    Golden Yellow with red hues
  • Body:
    Light-Medium
  • ABV:
    5.0%
  • IBU:
    15
